üìñ How to Play

üéØ Objective

Match all pairs of cards in the fewest moves and shortest time possible.

üéÆ How to Play

  1. Click on any card to flip it and reveal the symbol
  2. Click on a second card to try to find its match
  3. If the cards match, they stay flipped. If not, they flip back
  4. Continue until all pairs are matched
  5. Try to complete the game in as few moves as possible!

üèÜ Scoring

  • Match: +10 points
  • Time bonus: Faster completion = more points
  • Move penalty: Fewer moves = higher score

üéöÔ∏è Difficulty Levels

  • Easy: 4x2 grid (8 cards, 4 pairs)
  • Medium: 4x4 grid (16 cards, 8 pairs)
  • Hard: 6x4 grid (24 cards, 12 pairs)
  • Expert: 8x4 grid (32 cards, 16 pairs)

üí° Tips

  • Try to remember the positions of cards you've seen
  • Start from the corners and work your way in
  • Use the hint button sparingly for better scores
  • Take breaks if you're stuck - fresh eyes help!
